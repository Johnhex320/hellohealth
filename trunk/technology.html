Technology

Power to the platform.

Behind the new business model for your practice is the robust, web-based Hello Health platform. Browser-based, you just set up your practice web site and our technology does the rest. Our health care platform features integrated functionality that reduces data entry time and a secure, single source documentation repository that puts all your clinical and administrative information in one place. Hello Health integrates communications and social technologies like email and mobile apps that make it easy for you to connect with your patients and build care teams. And Hello Health includes an EHR that will meet the upcoming Meaningful Use certification standards, qualifying you for federal.

It’s designed with an intuitive interface that works the way you do. And the user experience for you and your patients is, quite simply, remarkable.

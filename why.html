Why Hello Health?

Think about it. What are you really looking for in your practice?

Maybe you’re looking to provide better access to care for your patients or looking to build more meaningful relationships with them to help them stay healthy. Maybe you’re looking to manage your practice more efficiently and effectively using technologies like we use every day for everything else. Maybe you’re looking for the freedom to choose how you practice within or outside of the insurance system. And, we are certain, you’re looking for ways to be compensated properly for all of the work you do and the value you create.

That’s where we come in.

We’ve created Hello Health to enable you to practice the way you truly want. Our technology is elegant in design. It integrates seamlessly with how you practice and with systems you have in place. It provides an effective pathway towards Meaningful Use. It enables you to build the networks your practice and your patients need. And when was the last time you heard a health care application described as beautiful? Well, Hello Health is.

But beyond all that, Hello Health is designed for doctors. We know you love your work. We also know that practicing today is tough, so we’ve built Hello Health with the flexibility to allow you to grow your ideal

practice.That’s why we say we’re the new business model for health care.

Your ideal model might be a direct-pay practice. Or a brand new practice.

Or a practice that captures the value of add-on services that your current insurance partners don’t cover. Whatever direction your vision takes you, Hello Health opens up whole new avenues of care, in your office and online. It gives you the freedom to see new kinds of revenue. It gives you the freedom to move at your speed into a better practice, and a better future.
